About Us

Dairy milk is a nutrient-rich liquid produced by mammals, primarily cows, and consumed by humans globally. It is an excellent source of essential nutrients, including calcium, vitamin D, protein, and potassium, which are vital for bone health, muscle function, and overall wellness. Dairy milk is available in various forms, such as whole, skimmed, and semi-skimmed, catering to different dietary preferences and health needs. It is commonly used in beverages, cooking, and baking. Despite its benefits, some people are lactose intolerant or allergic, prompting them to seek alternative milk options like almond, soy, or oat milk.
